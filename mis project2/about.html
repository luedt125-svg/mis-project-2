Who are the BizDogs?

BizDogs is a premier Living and Learning Community (LLC) specifically curated for high-achieving pre-business students.

What do the BizDogs do?

We bridge the gap between the classroom and the boardroom through immersive professional experiences.

Professional Growth

Guest instructors and industry experts host deep-dive workshops on networking and leadership.

Build a Strong Academic and Social Community

Engage in collaborative learning experiences and meaningful connections with peers and faculty.

Celebrate Each Other's Successes

Share achievements and milestones together, fostering a culture of recognition and support.
